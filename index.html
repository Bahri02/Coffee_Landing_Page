CAFE AU LAIT by admin 12/6/2021

Café au Lait: The French version of a latte. The term Café Au Lait is French for “coffee to the milk” and it is a popular traditional morning drink from France. It is made with steamed milk and double strength, dark roast coffee in a 50:50 ratio. 50% steamed milk, 50% coffee. Foam is generally not a feature of this drink but you can float a little foam on top if you want.

Le café au lait est une boisson chaude réalisée à partir de café et de lait . La première personne documentée buvant du café avec du lait est Jan Nieuhof en 1660, alors qu’il était ambassadeur néerlandais en Chine .

tasty and refreshing coffee By Admin 12/6/2021

Espresso MochaCaffè Mocha is a shot of espresso, milk and chocolate added, typically in the form of sweet cocoa powder. Chocolate will be added in 2 forms, either hot chocolate or chocolate syrup. Mochas can contain dark or milk chocolate.

Similar to cappuccino, caffè mochas contains distinctive milk foam on top. It is common with hot chocolate, but sometimes served with whipped cream instead.

Caffè Mocha usually topped with a dusting of either cinnamon or cocoa powder and marshmallows may also be added on top for flavor and decoration
